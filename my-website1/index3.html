About Anime

Inuyasha (Japanese: 戦 国 お 伽 草 子 -) is an anime and adventure-thriller manga series written by illustrator Remiko Takahashi. The series consists of two parts, the first part 167 episodes and the second part 26 episodes, the duration of the episode is 24 minutes. It was produced by the Japanese company Sunrise in 2000, it was shown on the Yumi Uri channel and continued to be broadcast in Japan in 2004. The name Inuyasha consists of two words, Inu, meaning dog and Yasha, which means monster Then the meaning of his name becomes Elf Dog. The series was dubbed into Arabic as (Enusha) and dubbed only 52 episodes, and it was shown on Space Power in 2008 and later on Space Toon in 2015 (after the closure of Space Power channel)
