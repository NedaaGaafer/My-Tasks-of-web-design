About anime

Naruto (Japanese: ナ ル and Rumaji: NARUTO) is the title of a finished Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Masashi Kishimoto. The story revolves around the teenage ninja Naruto Uzumaki, who found himself rejected by the inhabitants of his village because of the kyubi inside him, so he set his sights on obtaining the title of Hokage, which is the title given to the leader of the village and the strongest ninja in it, to win the respect and recognition of everyone. The manga was first released in Japan in 1999 by Shoesha Corporation in the forty-third issue of its weekly Shonen Jump. It is mainly adapted from Kishimoto's One-Shot comic and published in August 1997 in Akamaru Jump magazine. So far, 70 volumes of manga have been released in Japan, and the 71st volume will be the last in the manga. Naruto series is the best-selling series in the world; About 100 million copies have been sold in Japan. Later, Tokyo TV and Piero Studios converted the manga into an anime and it was broadcast for the first time in Japan on Tokyo TV on October 3, 2002. The first part of the anime was released under the name Naruto and consists of 220 episodes, and the second part of it came under the name Naruto Shippuden, which began broadcasting in February 15, 2007 The number of episodes of this part reached 500 episodes at the end. In addition to that, 9 films were released for the Naruto series and several episodes of Ufa. Other products related to this series have also been released and include short novels, video games and exchange cards.
