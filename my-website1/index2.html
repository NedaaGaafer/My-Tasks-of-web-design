About Anime

Attack on Titan (Japanese: 進 撃 の Shinveki no Kyujin ?, literally "the attacking giant") or Attack on Titan (English: Attack on Titan), a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Hajime Isayama. Its events take place in a fictional world where humans live within lands surrounded by three huge walls that protect them from giants who eat people. The events begin when one of the walls is breached, which is the Wall of Maria, where the giants exterminate a third of humanity.

Eren Yēgā

He is the main protagonist of the series of the novel. He dedicates his life to the elimination of giants after witnessing his mother being devoured by a giant, and after graduating from the fifth class of the army, he is devoured by a giant with a beard during his first mission in the Trust, but he emerged unscathed in the form of a 15-meter giant
